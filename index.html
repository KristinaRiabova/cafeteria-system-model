About the Cafeteria

A modern campus food service, not just a canteen

This cafeteria is designed as a digital-first service for a busy university campus. Students can order meals before class breaks, customize ingredients, check allergens, pay online, and collect food after it is prepared. Staff members use the system to manage preparation and reduce waiting time, while administrators update menus and monitor service quality.

The concept is creative because it turns a simple homework scenario into a full product story. At the same time, it remains realistic, because these features already exist in modern food ordering platforms and can be adapted for a university environment.
